Your finest resource for new construction clean-up in Central Texas.  We are a local, family owned business located in Waco, Texas, serving McLennan and neighboring counties within Central Texas. We take pride in our service and quality, doing it right the first time on-time.

 

You can trust Starboard Services to fulfill all of your expectations for all of your new home constriction cleaning.

Our fully trained staff will be given a detailed checklist of what is to be completed room by room. If during the cleaning process, we find any issues that need to be brought to your attention they will be noted and sent to you in an email.

Our basic cleaning includes the following.

Remove any interior debris

Dust all walls, ceilings, light fixtures, and ceiling fans

Dust and clean all vents and registers

Remove all cobwebs

Dust walls

Dust and clean the top of all cabinets and shelves, and ledges

Dust and clean all door and window frames and moldings

Clean all light switches and electrical outlets

Remove any stickers and glue from windows, mirrors, fixtures, showers, tubs, sinks, toilets, etc

Clean behind and under of any appliances that are movable

Clean the interior and exterior of all appliances

Clean sinks, tubs, showers, and toilets

Clean inside the medicine cabinets, built-in cabinets, drawers, under the sink, and vanities

Clean all mirrors

Clean shower doors and tracks

Clean shower heads, faucets

Clean counter tops and backsplashes

Clean and sanitize any soap dishes and toothbrush holders

Clean all doors

Clean all windows interior

Sweep, mop and vacuum all floors

Sweep the garage

Final quality check
