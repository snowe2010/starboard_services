Post Construction Cleaning

Our fully trained and dedicated staff follow a rigid checklist detailing every inch of the property to be cleaned. If, during the cleaning process, we find any issues that need to be brought to your attention you will be immediately notified.

Our basic post-construction interior cleaning starts with a rough clean followed by a detailed clean. The rough clean involves:

  • The rough clean involves:
    • Removal of large debris and trash
    • Removal of any stickers and glue from windows, mirrors, fixtures, showers, tubs, sinks, toilets, etc.
    • Wipe down of the surfaces in the kitchen bathrooms and cabinets inside and out
    • Sweeping of all flooring
  • The detailed clean consists of:
    • Removal of any interior debris
    • Dusting of all walls, ceilings, light fixtures, and ceiling fans
    • Dusting and cleaning of all vents and registers
    • Removal of all cobwebs
    • Dusting of walls
    • Dusting and cleaning of the top of all cabinets, shelves, and ledges
    • Dusting and cleaning of all door and window frames and moldings
    • Cleaning of all light switches and electrical outlets
    • Cleaning behind and beneath any appliances that are movable
    • Cleaning of the interior and exterior of all appliances
    • Cleaning of the inside of medicine cabinets, built-in cabinets, drawers, under sinks, and vanities
    • Cleaning of all mirrors
    • Cleaning of sinks, tubs, showers, and toilets
    • Cleaning of shower doors and tracks
    • Cleaning of shower heads and faucets
    • Cleaning of countertops and backsplashes
    • Cleaning and sanitization of any soap dishes
    • Cleaning of all doors
    • Cleaning of all window interiors
    • Sweeping, mopping, and vacuuming of all floors
    • Sweeping of garages
    • Final quality check
